WELCOME

Marshall McLuhan was right: the medium becomes the message when communication truly lands. Charities need the right tools to communicate mission, need, and impact — and to steward lasting donor relationships.

iShare® is an intuitive Virtual Reality platform for charities that activates engagement with stakeholders — building trust, fostering transparency, and nurturing long-term supporter relationships.

iShare® transforms your case for support into an immersive experience. Using AI-powered digital twinning, donors can step into your buildings and plans — visualizing spaces in vivid detail.

Key engagement tools include:

• AI-driven videos — brief, engaging explanations of each space (need, function, purpose).
• Affordability calculator — helps donors evaluate naming opportunities.
• Interactive exploration — donors navigate giving options and opportunities inside the experience.

Your personalized iShare® microsite integrates your case for support and guides donors to areas of interest — turning interest into informed, meaningful giving.
